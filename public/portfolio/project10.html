During my second and third years of study, I played an active role in the development of the solar boat project. However, in my fourth year, I took a step back from it. Towards the end of that year, I was asked to assist with addressing an issue related to the boat's rudder.

As we had discovered during the summer of 2022, the rudder couldn't withstand the stresses of operating on hydrofoils. Surprisingly, during a test run in 2023, we found that the rudder also couldn't handle the stresses of regular sailing. To address this problem, we improvised a solution in the final week before a race. We worked on a new rudder design, reinforcing the weaker parts by adding continuous carbon strips at various locations to create stronger connections.

The tests confirmed that the new rudder was strong enough. However, a new problem emerged. During the lamination process of the new rudder, a crucial step was overlooked, causing the rudder to be non-waterproof. This led to significant issues during the 2023 championship in Monaco.
