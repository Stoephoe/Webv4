For my minor at the RMI, I worked on a final project involving a mini TransAt boat. During this assignment, our group collaborated to construct a miniature sailboat. The Mini TransAt is a race in which model boats must autonomously cross the Atlantic Ocean.

Throughout this project, we followed the entire process from the initial design to the final production. Due to the pandemic, I completed a significant portion of the work from home. During the project, I focused on designing the boat. The hull shape was created using Rhino, and we conducted analyses in Maxsurf to optimize its form. The boat featured twin rudders, a daggerboard, and a canting keel. All components were designed with 3D printing in mind.

Ultimately, we designed everything so that the boat could be fully assembled within 2 days. The boat was equipped with a remote control. In the end, we emerged as winners of the intercollegiate student competition with this boat.
