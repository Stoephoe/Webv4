In the year when we were building the solar boat, a pandemic was affecting our progress at times, preventing us from participating in a race due to various circumstances.

Because of my substantial contributions in the previous year, I was invited to join the Monaco Energy Boat Challenge in 2022. My primary role there involved assisting with logistical tasks. In 2022, we entered a competition for the first time with our new boat, and during this competition, we achieved a respectable 7th place finish.
